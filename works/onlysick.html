PROJECT 02

ONLYSICK Apparel Graphics

Project Type Apparel Graphics
Duration 4 Weeks
Focus Area Streetwear / Music Culture
Deliverables Hand-Drawn T-Shirt Graphics / Print Assets / Collection Visuals

The Challenge

Onlysick set out to create a streetwear label that reflected the energy of today's music culture and resonated with Gen Z. The challenge was to transform the emotion, identity, and influence of globally recognized artists into original hand drawn apparel graphics that felt expressive without becoming conventional fan merchandise. Every piece needed to capture cultural relevance while establishing a visual identity unique to the brand.

The Solution

We developed a collection of hand illustrated graphics inspired by the emotion and visual language surrounding contemporary music culture. By combining expressive sketches, bold compositions, and refined streetwear aesthetics, each design translated the atmosphere of iconic artists into wearable storytelling. Close collaboration and continuous refinement ensured every release remained culturally relevant, visually distinctive, and aligned with Onlysick's vision of creating fashion that feels as impactful as the music behind it.

Client Feedback

They helped us glow up from basic merch to culturewear that speaks louder than words. The drops sold out quick like lightning, and the hype was insane. Can’t wait to do more lit stuff together!”
