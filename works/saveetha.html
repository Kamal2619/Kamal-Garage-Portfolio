PROJECT 04

LYNX 25 Saveetha Medical College

Project Type Graphic Design Strategy
Duration 1 Week
Focus Area Event Visuals & Garments
Deliverables Logo, Collateral, 240 Jerseys

The Challenge

Saveetha Medical College’s annual cultural event, LYNX 25, approached us to overhaul their graphic design strategy and elevate the event’s visual game. Previous years relied heavily on generic, web-sourced visuals that failed to create a cohesive or memorable identity. The media team wanted original, hand-drawn designs that would make a lasting impression across all event materials. The challenge was to create a strong, unique hand-drawn visual identity for LYNX 25, designing the logo, posters, brochures, certificates, and accompanying artwork with total originality. The goal was to break away from overused designs, build hype for the event, and strengthen engagement. We also needed to manufacture and deliver approximately 240 custom sports jerseys for the college sports team, ensuring best pricing and rapid turnaround.

The Solution

We produced a fully hand-sketched logo and visual suite on iPad, with every element crafted for authenticity and impact. All event collateral was designed and illustrated with painstaking attention to detail, replacing stock or repurposed graphics with a cohesive visual language that defined the event’s renewed identity and amplified its presence. By collaborating closely with the media team, we ensured swift feedback and alignment so the designs resonated with the audience. Additionally, we leveraged established custom garmenting networks to manufacture 240 high-quality sports jerseys at the best price, delivering all goods within one week and meeting tight timelines without compromise.

Client Feedback

We appreciate their dedication and look forward to future collaborations"
