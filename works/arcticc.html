PROJECT 01

ARCTICC

Project Type Brand Systems
Duration 4 Weeks
Focus Area Design / Research & Development
Deliverables Brand Identity / Hoodie Graphics / Design System / Visual Guidelines

The Challenge

ARCTICC set out to create more than a fashion brand it aimed to build a visual identity rooted in the spirit of Canada. The challenge was to translate themes of northern resilience, multiculturalism, and belonging into premium streetwear that felt timeless rather than trend driven. Every garment needed to tell a story while remaining understated, wearable, and emotionally meaningful.

The Solution

We developed a narrative-driven design system built on research, symbolism, and refined minimalism. Every graphic, color, and typographic element was chosen to reinforce the brand's connection to Canadian identity without relying on obvious clichés. Through carefully crafted storytelling, restrained luxury, and iterative refinement, we created a cohesive collection that transformed everyday hoodies into meaningful cultural statements establishing ARCTICC as a brand defined by quiet confidence, authenticity, and enduring design.
