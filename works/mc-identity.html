PROJECT 06

MC STUDIO Identity

Project Type In-house Brand
Duration Ongoing
Focus Area Aesthetic Direction
Deliverables Brand Universe

The Challenge

We needed a studio identity that was the ultimate representation of our design philosophy: raw mechanical precision, high contrast visual elements, and zero safe plays.

The Solution

The Minimal Creates identity features a geometric 3D wireframe cube alongside bold, custom sans-serif typography. It acts as our playground for experimental branding systems.
